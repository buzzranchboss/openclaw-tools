# MWS TikTok Caption Standard

## Format
[Hook line — 1 sentence, punchy, speaks to the video subject]

[3-5 high-impact hashtags — mix of broad reach + subject-specific, NO brand/product/city tags]

## Rules
- Hook must be conversational, first-person or observational — not corporate
- No mention of: Memphis, MWS, OpenClaw, or any specific AI model names (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.)
- Hashtags: always include #AI + 2-3 subject-specific high-traffic terms + 1 broad trend tag
- No "Caption:" or "Hashtags:" labels — just the text, ready to paste
- Adapt hook and hashtags to the specific video subject every time

## Example (AI guardrails video)
I kept asking my AI the same question until it figured out how to fix itself. Here's what we built. 🤖

#AI #ArtificialIntelligence #AITools #MachineLearning #FutureOfWork
